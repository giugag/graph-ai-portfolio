About Me

Victor Meza is an undergraduate student at the University of California, Davis, pursuing a B.S. in International Agriculture Development. He is passionate about the intersection of technology and sustainable agriculture.

Victor has hands-on experience managing a successful online plant shop, Plants4You, and volunteering at the Knights Landing One Health Center. His skills span Microsoft Office, data management, and bilingual communication in English and Tagalog.

He is currently expanding his technical expertise by studying Generative AI applications for research productivity and data analysis.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an immersive, 8-week live cohort program designed by the GRAPH Network, University of Geneva. The course introduces participants to practical, no-code AI tools for automating research workflows, analyzing data, and enhancing scientific communication.
